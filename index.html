We are a team of students, researchers and developers based at the AGH University of Krakow in Poland. Our activities concern open-source software for atmospheric sciences, and cloud physics in particular. We develop open, example-rich tools to support reproducible computational research, while promoting best practices in research software engineering. Our team contributes to the open-source ecosystem we rely on. We explore modern, transferable technologies that are useful both within and beyond academia. Most of the projects we develop, maintain and contribute to are hosted at github.com/open-atmos, hence the open-atmos-krk name.
